Tommy's Chili

A taste of 4000 orth rd. My dad spent a greater part of his adult life perfecting his piano playing and his chilli recipe. From my home to yours. I hope you enjoy the comfort and warmth of a big ol bowl of Tommy J's chilli.

Prep Time:

30 minutes

Cook Time:

2 hours

Total Time:

2.5 hours

Dietary Restrictions:

Gluten free, Vegan if no meat or sub beyond beef

Ingredients

    Sautee Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground sirloin (optional)
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped red pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery

    • Stewed Ingredients

  • 3 28 ounce stew tomato (del monte is best)
  • 1 14 1/2 ounce stew tomato
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon basil
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon cumin

    • Simmer Ingredients

  • 3 cup fajita mix
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2/3 cup ketchup
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 tablespoon parsley flakes
  • 1 tablesppon cilanro flakes
  • 1 15 oz Kuner butter beans
  • 1 21.5 oz bush grillin beans sweet mesquite
  • 1 15 oz kidney beans
  • 1 8 oz bush baked beans
  • 1 8 oz pinto beand

    • Optional Ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • sour cream
  • Shredded cheese

Materials

  • Large Frying pan
  • Large pot
  • Large wooden spoon
  • measuring cups
  • measuring spoons

How to Make

Take ground sirloin, fry then chop and set aside.Save juice

Sautee 1 c. chopped onions, 1 c. chopped red pepper and 1/2 c. choppd celary and sautee in ground sirloin juice.

In very large pot add stewed tomatoes, pepper, salt, basil, oregano, garlic, cumin, sauteed veggies..

Cook on very low for one hour.

Mix in ground sirloin, beans, and all other remaining ingredients

now heat on low for 30 minutes.

Serve warm with tortilla chips, sourcream, shredded cheese, or all the above for extra delicousness! Now go enjoy

Tommy Tips

  • Tastes even better next day!
  • Left overs? Portion out and freeze them in zip locks! Thaw under warm water and empty baggy contents into a pan to reheat for a quick and easy meal!
